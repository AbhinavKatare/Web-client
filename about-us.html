About Pragya Trust

Pragya Trust is a non-profit organization, registered in India, dedicated to the exploration of consciousness through the lens of Sanatan Hindu Dharma. Our mission is to provide a platform for people from all walks of life to explore and experience the practical applications of this ancient wisdom.

Our Approach

We believe that direct experience is the most powerful way to understand the deeper layers of our existence. Our approach is grounded in the six steps of Sahajawatsha, Dhyan dharana, Yog Pranayam, Jap, Swadhyay n Path, and Yatra. We offer courses, workshops, and retreats that are designed to guide participants through these steps, helping them to cultivate a deeper understanding of themselves and the world around them.

Our History

Pragya Trust was founded in 2015 by Dr. Jyotika Saini, a renowned psychologist and spiritual teacher. Dr. Saini has dedicated her life to the study of consciousness and its impact on human experience. Her vision for Pragya Trust is to create a global community of conscious explorers who are committed to personal growth and collective evolution.

Our Impact & Achievements

Since its inception, Pragya Trust has impacted the lives of thousands of individuals around the world. Our courses have been instrumental in helping people to overcome limiting beliefs, heal from past traumas, and develop a deeper sense of purpose. We have also worked with organizations to integrate the principles of Sanatan Hindu Dharma into their corporate cultures, resulting in improved employee well-being and performance.

In addition to our educational programs, we are also committed to social impact initiatives. We have partnered with local communities to provide access to mental health services in underserved areas, and we have launched several environmental conservation projects to promote sustainability and eco-friendly practices.
